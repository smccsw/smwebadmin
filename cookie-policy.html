Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and to collect basic analytical data.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences.

What cookies do we use?

Essential cookies: Required for basic functionality of the website (e.g. navigation, session management).

Analytics cookies: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. These cookies collect information anonymously.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you refuse to accept cookies and delete existing ones.
