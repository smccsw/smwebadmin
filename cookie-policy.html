Cookie Policy

We only use optional analytics cookies if you choose to accept them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences.

What cookies do we use?

Essential: This website is a simple brochure site and does not require login/session cookies for basic browsing.

Analytics (optional): If you accept analytics cookies, we use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are visited and how long people stay).

Google Analytics may set cookies such as _ga and _ga_* to help measure usage. These identifiers are controlled by Google Analytics.

You can accept or reject analytics cookies via the cookie banner. You can also change your choice at any time:

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you refuse to accept cookies and delete existing ones.
