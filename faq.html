Frequently asked questions

Short answers to common questions. For anything specific, get in touch.

Who is SM Web & Admin for?

We mainly work with small businesses, sole traders, and local services on the Isle of Man and wider UK who want a clear, professional website — not a complicated system to manage. See the Who we can help section on the home page for a fuller picture.

What is a brochure website?

A brochure-style site focuses on explaining what you do, building trust, and helping visitors get in touch. It is a good fit for many small businesses before they need heavier features like large product catalogues or member areas.

Do you handle domain names and hosting?

Yes. We can help you keep your domain and hosting in your name, set up reliably, so you stay in control and are not boxed into a platform you cannot escape. More detail appears under What we offer on our home page.

Can you add e-commerce, bookings, or similar later?

Often, yes. We usually start simple when that matches the business; when it makes sense we can integrate tools such as a small shop, payments, or booking flows, planned step by step so the site stays easy to understand.

How do content updates work after launch?

We aim to keep day-to-day changes straightforward — you tell us what needs updating or we agree a sensible approach for tweaks. The goal is useful updates without turning your site into something you dread opening.

How much does a website cost?

It depends on scope: number of pages, design needs, integrations, copy, imagery, and hosting setup. After a short, plain-English conversation we can outline options and pricing so you know what you are paying for.

Where are you based, and how do we get started?

SM Web & Admin is Isle of Man based and works with clients locally and further afield where it is a good fit. The easiest next step is to get in touch by phone, email, or WhatsApp via the Contact section on our home page.
